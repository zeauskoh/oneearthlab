Novel Inventions

source

we go down to neighbourhood shops and supermarkets around singapore to buy imperfect produce.

partner

we partner with restaurants and supermarkets to buy ingredients about to expire.

cook

in shifts, we prepare cakes, snacks or light meals that can be easily distributed. we do this every fortnight.

distribution

we then coordinate with our partner centres, such as community kitchens and homeless shelters, to deliver the food to the needy.
